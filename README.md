Echoes of Survival is a **procedural, choice-driven survival game** built in Python.  
Each playthrough generates a unique narrative experience where **every decision impacts your survival and ending**.

The game focuses on **fast-paced gameplay**, **high replayability**, and **meaningful choices**, inspired by roguelike and narrative survival games.

---

## üéÆ Game Overview

You are stranded in a hostile environment with limited resources.  
Each turn presents a situation that forces you to make a decision.

Your choices affect:
- Health
- Hunger
- Energy
- Morale
- Reputation

Survive long enough, and your decisions will shape how your story ends.
